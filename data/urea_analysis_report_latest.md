## 1. Executive Summary

The African urea trade data reveals a continent heavily dependent on imports to meet its nitrogen fertilizer needs, with South Africa dominating imports ($323M in 2024), followed by Ethiopia ($489M in 2023) and Nigeria (historically significant but declining post-2015). The data reveals a continent in transition, with several countries developing domestic production capacity while others remain heavily import-dependent for food security.
